Insturctions

This is how you play this number guessing game. You will be attempting to guess the number based on the range you choose. If youâ€™re incorrect, you may ask for a hint. The hint will tell you whether the number is higher or lower. In order to play, first type in the lowest number for your range. Next, type in the highest number for your range. Then you will be asked if you want a hint. You may type yes or no. Finally, if you believe you got the number, type it in the shell.
